About Us

Welcome to Environmagazine! We are a daily Environmental magazine. We focus on current events, and tailor our weekly mix to you.

Founding- We were founded in 2016 by activist and CEO of EnviroTek, Naomi Goldstein. A small newsletter was sent out to Newton, and, eventually, the business grew until a web designer and photographer were hired, and business boomed. We now have an extremely large cult following, and we have impacted the environment by suggesting change to our readers.

Changes- Please look look at your community everyday and try to make a change. If you email us about what you did at changes@environmagazine.com, we may write a feature article about you.

If you would like to volunteer, we have contacts with Amazon Rainforest cleanup company- Cleanup Forest, so please go to their website at cleanupforest.org
