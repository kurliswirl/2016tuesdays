Womens rights and gender equality Information for Women's Rights Website:
  • Only 7,000 out of 500,000 computing jobs in the United States are occupied women.
  • In 2015, women earned only 18% of all computer science degrees in the nation.
  • Overall, women earn 70 cents to every dollar that men earn.
  • Women in most countries earn on average only 60 to 75% of men's wages.
  • 603 million women live in countries where domestic violence is not yet considered a crime.
  • Women make up 80% of all refugees and displaced people.
  • Women gained the right to vote in America in 1920, after African-American men got the right to vote.
  • 62 million girls are denied an education all over the world.
  • In some places, women aren't allowed to drive and are discouraged from working jobs that would put them in contact with men.
  • The unemployment rate for women is 34 percent for women, 7 percent for men.
